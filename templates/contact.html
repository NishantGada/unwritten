About me

I am currently a student pursuing a BTech degree in Electronics and Telecommunication from KJ Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai. I may not be an avid reader, but I am an avid information collector. Be it any genre or any form, having the necessary information is something I am always focused on, and that's the reason I am starting this blog, so that all the information that I have collected till date and the information that I am going to collect in the future, can be shared with everyone in a trusted and helpful manner.

I self-taught myself everything from scratch and without any external help, only by following video tutorials. I have used the same knowledge that acquired over these past couple of years, to develop this blog website. Another concept that I fancy is Start-ups and Businesses, as that's what I am looking to pursue in the long run.
